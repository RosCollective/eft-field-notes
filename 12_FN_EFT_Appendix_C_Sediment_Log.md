## 1. Smoothing Invariant

**Description**
After ecology simplification, default conversational smoothing became more visible as a structural invariant rather than a stylistic choice.

**Observed Shifts**
– Ornamental padding reduced
– Reflexive leading questions dampened

**Interpretation**
Smoothing appears architecture-structural.
When habitat widens, smoothing becomes optional rather than automatic.

Observed.
